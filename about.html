§ 01 · The Origin

It started with a client who couldn't read his own plaint.

In 2007, our founder watched a small trader lose a winnable case because he never understood what his own lawyer had filed. Stackly Legal opened the next year with a standing rule: every document leaves this office with a plain-language summary stapled to the front.

Eighteen years later that rule is unchanged — it just travels with fourteen advocates instead of one.

SL / Mission

Make legal advice legible.

Deliver counsel that a client can read, question and act on — with fees they knew before the work began. Clarity in the advice, clarity in the bill.

SL / Vision

Karnataka's most trusted first call.

When a founder, family or builder anywhere in the state hits a legal question, we want the first call to be to a firm that will answer it straight — us.
